    All CT Exams with IV & Oral Contrast

    Nothing to eat 4hrs. Prior to exam time
    Nothing to drink 2hrs. Prior to exam time

    ***TAKE ALL MEDICATION AS PRESCRIBED***

    CT Abdomen:
    Drink/finish 1 bottle of oral prep (cheetah/redi-cat) one 1hr. prior to the exam time.

    CT abdomen/pelvis:
     Drink/finish 1 bottle of oral prep (cheetah/redi-cat) 3hrs. prior to the exam time.
    Drink/finish the 2nd bottle of oral prep (cheetah/redi-cat) 1hr. prior to the exam time

    Labs required if:
     1. You are 60 years of age or older
    2. You have known or potential renaldisease.
    3. You are a diabetic.
    Patient to complete lab work ordered by requesting provider/pcp
    Renal function (blood test) must be done 1 week prior to exam date

    Films:
    Patients to bring non sma cat scan films related to the exam on scheduled date
    Failure to complete lab work may result in your appointment being rescheduled.

    Do not bring children with you to your appointment. We do not have the facilities or the personnel to provide for their care while your exam is being done. This could cause your appointment to be rescheduled.

    Imaging recommends not wearing jewelry to your scheduled appointment.
